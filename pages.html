radioactive nightmare:project Chernobyl

Radioactive Nightmare: Project Chernobyl 26 April 1986. A manmade mistake would lead to the biggest ecological disaster which would make the name "Chernobyl" shiver for many generations. Serghei Ilici, a scientist, had the fortune to survive the explosion while many of colleagues died in vain. Thirty years later, Serghei receives a message from a relative to meet him in what used to be his home, Pripyat - the heart of the Exclusion Zone. He goes there just to discover the severe effects of the radiation. The entire land is an undead limbo and he must escape from there. We proudly present the survival-horror video game which will define the 8th generation of gaming. Coming soon on your PlayStation 4, Xbox One, Wii U and Microsoft Windows. It features over 5 hours of gameplay to explore the whole Chernobyl Exclusion zone, a downright creepy and deep story, an ambiental soundtrack which gives you the chillies and great graphics that push the consoles to their limits. It was first mentioned in 1997 and it was developed for PlayStation and Nintendo 64. Later, a Sega Dreamcast was in development before our previous company got bankrupt in 2004. The project has been revived and we hope that it will materialize in what we expected from the beginning.

litu(leap into the unknown)

LITU(Leap Into The Unknown) As the technology advances people start to expand their legacy throughout the universe. That meant even colonizing new galaxies. You and a few other explorers have discovered a new planet with the same atmosphere as the Earth in the Andromeda Galaxy. This game combines the Final Fantasy-like RPG gameplay with an esence of SimCity and has immersive design and a diversity of actions and ecosystems. Preferably to be released on online stores such as Steam. Features to be included:

  • Real time events which influence the weather
  • Almost infinite jungles and wastelands
  • Socialize with other colonizers
  • Multiplayer Online
  • Crafting
  • RPG fights with levels
Your mission is to make that planet inhabitable and make sure that your new home develops peacefully and has perfect conditions for other colonists. Also, you can advance through a level system and improve your strength, intelligence, health and other special abilities. Defend your settlements from hostile creatures and space pirates.

city of fortune

City Of Fortune , Bucharest, Romania. It is November 1999. A new millenium full of prosperity and hope is coming. The biggest Gypsy mafia family in the city lost their leader after a surprise attack led by gangsters from Vaslui. One possible succesor, Johnny T, has recently been released from prison after 10 years just to see his clan's decline. He swears revenge on those who had murdered their boss and tries to reclaim his status in the city before the turn of the new millenium. DarkBlocks Studios presents an open-world adventure video game promised as a Grand Theft Auto V-like redux which include dozens of new features such as QuickTime events and real time events. Coming soon on your PlayStation 4, Xbox One and Microsoft Windows. Originally started as a mission-based shooter game on PlayStation 2 in 2011 it became an open-world adventure game for the PlayStation 3 and Xbox 360. The latter had been cancelled due to Microsoft's indie developer policy. Th PlayStation 3 version has been left unfinished. As the PS4 arrived on market, the PS3 has been considered unviable. However a prototype still exists in the archive.
